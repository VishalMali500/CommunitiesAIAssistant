

1. Standardizing our Tech Stack for 2026
Posted by: Sarah Chen (Director of Engineering) | Date: Jan 15, 2026, 09:15 AM

Post:
As we scale, the "fragmentation tax" is becoming real. Currently, we have 14 different frontend frameworks across 20 teams. While I love autonomy, our cross-team mobility is suffering. I’m proposing a "Golden Path" for new services: Next.js for web, Go for high-concurrency microservices, and Python for data-heavy tasks. Thoughts on where we should draw the line?

Sarah Chen (Jan 15, 01:00 PM): Agreed, Aisha. The goal is to make the right choice the easy choice, not the only choice.
